Responsible Gambling

Last Updated:

1. Our Commitment

At Review5Star, we promote safe and responsible gambling practices. While gambling can be entertaining, it's important to recognize it carries risks.

2. Warning Signs

Be aware of problematic gambling behaviors:

  • Spending more money or time than intended
  • Chasing losses or gambling to escape problems
  • Neglecting work, family, or personal responsibilities
  • Borrowing money to gamble
  • Hiding gambling activities from loved ones

3. Self-Help Tools

Most licensed casinos offer these responsible gambling features:

  • Deposit Limits: Set daily/weekly/monthly deposit amounts
  • Loss Limits: Control maximum loss amounts
  • Wager Limits: Restrict betting amounts
  • Time-Outs: Take short breaks from gambling (24h-6 weeks)
  • Self-Exclusion: Longer-term exclusion options (6 months+)

4. Protecting Vulnerable Players

We recommend:

  • Keeping gambling accounts secure
  • Not gambling while under the influence
  • Avoiding gambling when emotionally distressed
  • Keeping gambling as entertainment, not income

5. Parental Controls

To prevent underage gambling:

  • Use device-level parental controls
  • Keep gambling account credentials private
  • Monitor payment methods
  • Educate about gambling risks

6. Support Organizations

If you need help, contact these organizations:

  • India: Gamblers Anonymous India
  • International: BeGambleAware
  • 24/7 Helpline: 1800-522-4700 (India)

7. Our Recommendations

For safer gambling:

  • Set strict budgets and stick to them
  • Balance gambling with other activities
  • Never chase losses
  • Take regular breaks
  • Monitor your time and spending
